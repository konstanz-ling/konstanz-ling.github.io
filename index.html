About

The Konstanz Linguistic Conference aims to provide early career researchers working in all fields of Linguistics an opportunity to get together in a relaxed atmosphere to present their research in oral and poster presentations and exchange ideas. The Konstanz Linguistics Conference will take place at the University of Konstanz on March 21-22, 2024.
